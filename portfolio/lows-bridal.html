Low's Bridal

About Low's Bridal

Low's Bridal began with six dresses in a room above the family pharmacy. 42 years later, Low's Bridal retains the personal boutique experience, while offering a selection of more than 2,500 wedding dresses. You can read more on their about page.

About The Project

Mission: Their previous website was built a long time ago. They needed to be on a more modern platform, with a fresh mobile responsive design. We also wanted to give them the ability to show product categories and individual products on their website. Phase 1 was built in a way where a more comprehensive quote system or ecommerence and checkout could be added in the future.

Technologies Used: Laravel, Tailwind CSS
